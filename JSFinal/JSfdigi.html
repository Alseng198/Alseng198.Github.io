Elementals Concept 1

Description

After writing over 120 pages of my first novel, I gathered the courage to draw the main character; Clive Bayker and his familiar Claire. Clive can use magic of all four elements; fire, air, water, and earth. In this image he is using air (green swirls) and fire. His familiar is a mythical snow leopard with wings named Claire. Together they protect and defend their fellow Elementals against the evil Warlocks; normal humans who want to study and experiment on them.
This piece was the first of a series of digital experiments. I had never done anything so detailed in Photoshop before, so I'm really happy with how it turned out.

Elementals Concept 2

Description

This is my second concept with Clive and Claire. This time I added in a very special character called Destiny. I wanted to focus on the amount of distrust Clive has with Destiny, so I placed Destiny with his back to us and gave Clive the appropriate expression.

Sorcerers Concept

Description

This is the main character of another novel I am writing. Silas is a sorcer with the ability to summon weapons. He was given the task to go undercover within the School of Sorcery to find the traitor amongst them. During the day he's just a normal student, but by night he's a full-fledge Sorcerer, fighting monsters and defending the weak. I wanted to show his day and night differences with day on the left and night on the right.
