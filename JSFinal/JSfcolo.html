About Me

Hello! I am Alseng, a
physical and digital artist.
I picked up drawing as a
hobby in 7th grade, starting
with realism portraits of my
favorite actors. From then on,
I've continued to grow
and hone my skills.
This page includes all of my
recent artwork ranging from
high school to now; my Junior
year at the University of Colorado
Boulder.

Color Filter Study 1 - Both

Description

This piece is the first of 2 for my first college art final. I wanted to try something completely new. I had seen a peer create a drawing with the same effect back in high school and was determined to make my own.
Using colored pencils on black paper, I drew this dragon and colored him with red and blue. In the next two images, you'll see the red filter and the blue filter.

Materials

Colored pencil on black paper

Year : 2022

Size: 18" x 24"

Color Filter Study 1 - Red

Description

This is the dragon with the red filter on. It shows every detail of the dragon.

Materials

Year : 2022

Size: 18" x 24"

Color Filter Study 1 - Blue

Description

This is the dragon with a blue filter on. This version was my main focus, making sure the red disappeared as much as possible so that the dragon's skeleton was clear.

Color Filter Study 2 - Both

Description

This is the second piece in my art final in color study. I was a lot more ambitious now that I knew how to get the right effect. Drawing almost a full-body image, I had much more difficulty in making sure that the skeleton was clear and filled the space it was supposed to. I also added new plants to add to the effect.
