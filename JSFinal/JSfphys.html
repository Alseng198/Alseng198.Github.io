Reality V.S. Virtual

Description

Taking my character Vivian from the novel that I am currently writing,
I chose to draw her in her two different worlds. The left being the real
world, where she isn't exactly 'grounded.' Meanwhile, the right is the
virtual world that she created, where she is in the scene herself and part
of their lives.
Using colored pencil to bring life and personality to the characters, I
was able to draw the viewer's eyes from the top left corner to the
bottom right. Thus, every aspect can be seen to its fullest.

Materials

Charcoal and colored pencil on toned paper.

Year : 2024

Size: 18" x 24"

Line Models

Description

Positive-Negative Study

Description

Using the shapes from my Line Models piece, I was told to create a
new piece that studies positive and negative spaces. So, I descided to
go a step further and study life V.S. metal. Using a flat checkerboard
on the metal pieces, and lively flowers on the living, I was able to pull
it off with curves lines on the bones for a middle ground.

Materials

A Study in White Charcoal

Description

This was done in my high school years, where I had a lot of freedom to
decide what to draw and which materials to use. Having always drawn
on white paper, I decided to try something new. Using black paper
and white charcoal, my world was suddenly upside down. This
character is Shima Renzou from the anime Blue Exorcist. I was trying
my hands at more cartoon-style drawings and Shima was my top
character at the time. I love how this piece turned out and will
definately do more white charcoal works in the future.

Materials

White charcoal on black paper

Year: 2019

Size: 14" x 11.5"
